Python is a high-level, interpreted programming language that is known for its simplicity, readability, and versatility. It is a popular language among developers due to its ease of use, extensive libraries, and extensive support from the Python community.

Python is a general-purpose programming language that is used for a wide range of applications, including web development, data analysis, artificial intelligence, and more. It is often used in conjunction with other languages, such as Java, C++, and C
